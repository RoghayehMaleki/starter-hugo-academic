Biography

Welcome to my web page. I am Roghayeh (Mitra) Maleki. I am a postdoctoral researcher in mathematics at the University of Primorska, working with Dr. Štefko Miklavič‬.

I completed my Ph.D in algebraic combinatorics at the University of Regina in 2022, under the supervision of Dr. Allen Herman.

My research interests lie in the area of algebraic combinatorics. I am particularly interested in the theory of association schemes and related combinatorial objects.

Research

My current research projects are the following.

  1. Terwilliger algebras and Association schemes
  2. The Cyclotomic Eigenvalue Question for commutative association schemes
  3. Intersection type problems.
